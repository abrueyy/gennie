Gennie

Welcome, meet Gennie.

Gennie is a small discord genie that can fulfill multiple wishes for your server. Moderating, playing music, verifying etc.

Information

Gennie is an newborn Discord bot with few helpfull features.
Adding him would make an great addition to your server.

(The bot's features wil grow with every update.)

Moderation

Keep your server safe with our moderation commands.

They will help moderators to take actions on bad users.

Music Commands

Ever wanted to listen to music with friends in the voice channel or just to chill.

The bot can fix this, it supports playlists and tracks from: YouTube, Spotify and SoundCloud.

Verify

Do you want to inplement a "verify role" on your server?

The bot is capable of so called verify button embed. This makes it way easier to inplement "verify role".

Rich Embed Builder

The bot offers you rich embed builder command.

A simple, clean command to help you build embed messages for your Discord server.

Server Templates

If you ever wanted to make your own community server.

Gennie has some free Discord server templates for you.
(You can find them using Gennie's commands.)
